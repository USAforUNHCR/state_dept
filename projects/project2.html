Solution: Virtual Distance Teacher Training

Concept:

​An attempt to address the issue of psychosocial challenges many children experience ­ not merely due to conflict alone but due to prevalent violence and bullying in school. Create a teacher training tool and curriculum that empowers teachers to foster inclusion and address trauma ­ and work in tandem teachers to equip them to meet their needs, and the evolving realities of the classroom.

Need:

These resources are not available and can provide a joint employment and training tool. A virtual training program working in communities of practice with other teachers in their region in face to face and virtually, building a cohort of support.

Partners:

​Varki Foundation’s master teacher training program; explore leveraging existing partnerships within the Teacher’s College at Columbia and online training videos from the Teaching Channel.

Solution(s) Proposed:

​A​​m​ulti­pronged approach, working from the ground up to make this platform available to teachers.

Key Milestones:

  • Engaging in partnerships with ministries of education to more formally integrate into schools.
