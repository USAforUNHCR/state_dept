Solution: Blended Online and In­-Person Education

Concept:

​A unified approach to online and in­-person education, the “online” approach will allow the initiative to scale, while in-­person education will allow administrators to provide personal touchstones young people need in the form of workshops, monitorships and courses at local universities.

Need:

T​here are no existing unified education platforms that seamlessly integrate online and in­-person education.

Potential Partners:

Investors, mentors, universities, NGOs (Jusoor, IIE).

Solution(s) Proposed:

​Business engagement and the involvement of the private sector writ large – it makes “good business sense” for individual investors to invest in young people.

Key Milestones:

Prototyping technology solutions leveraging universities, investors and test cohort of students – issuing a global call for universities to offer free online degrees.

  • Universities will offer free online courses in partnership with Joosur – and might allow access to a database of free programs for in-person communication.
